Little Boracay Calatagan

Little Boracay in Calatagan, Batangas is a stunning beach destination that has earned its nickname due to its resemblance to the famous Boracay Island. It offers pristine white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and a serene atmosphere that attracts both locals and tourists seeking a tranquil getaway.

Welcome to Little Boracay

This enchanting destination captivates visitors with its pristine white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and tranquil ambiance reminiscent of its more famous counterpart, Boracay Island. Little Boracay offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, inviting travelers to unwind amidst stunning natural beauty. Whether you're seeking a relaxing beach retreat, adventurous water sports, or simply breathtaking sunsets, Little Boracay in Calatagan promises an unforgettable experience for all who venture to its shores.

How to go to Little Boracay?

Unlike Boracay which is famous for its long white beach, Little Boracay is in the middle of the sea. You will need to get a floating house or a floating cottage to get there. Your floating house or cottage will be pulled by a boat to your destination
