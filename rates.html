How to go to Little Boracay?

Unlike Boracay which is famous for its long white beach, Little Boracay is in the middle of the sea. You will need to get a floating house or a floating cottage to get there. Your floating house or cottage will be pulled by a boat to your destination

Our Floating Cottage

A floating cottage can take 10 pax. Price for normal cottage is 3,500 and for floating cottage with view deck is 4,500. Children below 3 years of age are free of charge. It can accommodate up to 15 pax with an additional 400 pesos per head. Strictly not more than 15 persons per cottage. The floating cottage is small enough that it can dock in shallow waters and on the shore. All providers of floating cottages are docked near Calatagan public market. Cottage would normally have a table and 2 benches, a view deck, a small room for storage and a grill. It will be pulled by a boat to your destination with 2 boatmen. Life vests are provided.
