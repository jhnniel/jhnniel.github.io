Destination

Come Aboard And Explore This Beautiful Four Destination

Get on board a floating cottage and enjoy a day out of the city. Discover 4 different spots on this area, Sand bar when the tide is low the sand bar emerges from the sea which is great for swimming with friends and family or just chill. Snorkelling area we then head to an area rich in marine life, snorkel equipment will be provided and life vest for a truly amazing experience. Starfish island this area has large numbers of starfish and will truly captivate your experience and lastly Little Boracay.

Sandbar

Sandbar Island

The first stop, the breathtaking beauty of our pristine sandbar. Imagine walking on soft, powdery sand surrounded by crystal-clear waters, where the only sound you hear is the gentle lapping of waves. Our sandbar offers a perfect retreat for beach lovers and nature enthusiasts alike.

Starfish

Starfish Island

The second stop is Starfish site, See the beauty of colorful starfish under the crystal water of little boracay. Starfish, also known scientifically as sea stars, are fascinating marine creatures found in oceans around the world.

Snorkeling

Snorkeling

Snorkel in crystal-clear waters teeming with vibrant marine life and see the beauty of coral reefs and tropical fish. Dive into adventure and explore the underwater wonders just beneath the surface. Join us for an unforgettable snorkeling experience!

Little Boracay

Little Boracay

And lastly, The little boracay the famous tourist attraction here in Batangas. It offers pristine white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and a serene atmosphere that attracts both locals and tourists seeking a tranquil getaway.
